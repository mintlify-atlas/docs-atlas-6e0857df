Your brand settings control how your Digital Product Passports appear to customers and how your organization operates within Avelero. This guide covers all the brand configuration options available.

## Accessing brand settings

To access your brand settings:

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar navigation
2. You'll see the main brand settings page with all configuration options

The settings page includes sections for logo, name, slug, email, country, custom domain, and brand deletion.

<Note>
  Some settings are only available to brand owners. If you're a team member, you may see limited options.
</Note>

## Brand identity settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your brand logo">
    Your logo appears on all Digital Product Passports and in the Avelero interface.
    
    **To upload or change your logo:**
    
    1. In the **Logo** section, click on the circular logo placeholder
    2. Select an image file from your computer
    3. The logo uploads automatically
    
    **Logo requirements:**
    - Recommended size: At least 400x400 pixels
    - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
    - Square images work best (will be displayed in a circle)
    - File size: Under 5MB
    
    <Info>
      Your logo is displayed in a circle, so square or circular logo designs work best. Rectangular logos will be cropped to fit.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your brand name">
    Your brand name appears throughout the platform and on customer-facing passports.
    
    **To update your brand name:**
    
    1. In the **Brand name** section, click **Edit**
    2. Enter your new brand name
    3. Click **Save**
    
    The brand name should be:
    - Your official company or brand name
    - At least 2 characters long
    - Clear and professional
    
    <Warning>
      Changing your brand name doesn't affect existing passport URLs, but it will appear differently to customers viewing your passports.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your brand slug">
    The brand slug is used in your passport URLs and should be memorable and relevant.
    
    **To set or change your slug:**
    
    1. In the **Brand slug** section, click **Edit**
    2. Enter your desired slug
    3. Click **Save**
    
    **Slug requirements:**
    - Lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only
    - No spaces or special characters
    - Must be unique across Avelero
    - Should be short and memorable
    
    **Example slugs:**
    - `acme-apparel`
    - `sustainable-goods`
    - `organic-textiles`
    
    <Info>
      Your slug appears in URLs like `avelero.com/brand/your-slug/product-handle`. Choose something that represents your brand well.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a contact email">
    The contact email is displayed on your Digital Product Passports, allowing customers to reach you.
    
    **To set your contact email:**
    
    1. In the **Email** section, click **Edit**
    2. Enter a customer-facing email address (e.g., support@yourbrand.com)
    3. Click **Save**
    
    <Note>
      Use an email address that your team monitors regularly, as customers may use it for product inquiries or feedback.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your country">
    The country setting indicates where your brand is based.
    
    **To set or change your country:**
    
    1. In the **Country** section, click **Edit**
    2. Select your country from the dropdown
    3. Click **Save**
    
    This information may be displayed on your passports and is used for regional compliance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Custom domain setup

Custom domains allow you to use your own domain for Digital Product Passport URLs, which is required for GS1-compliant QR codes.

<Info>
  Custom domains are only available to brand owners. Team members won't see this option.
</Info>

### Why use a custom domain?

**Brand consistency**
- Passport URLs use your domain (e.g., `products.yourbrand.com/item-123`)
- Builds trust with customers who see your familiar domain

**GS1 compliance**
- Required for GS1 Digital Link QR codes
- Enables standardized product identification

**Professional appearance**
- Your brand owns the entire customer experience
- No third-party branding in URLs

### Setting up a custom domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the custom domain configuration">
    1. In the **Custom Domain** section, click **Configure**
    2. The custom domain modal will open
    
    If you already have a domain configured, you'll see its current status (Verified, Pending, or Verification Needed).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your domain">
    1. Enter the subdomain you want to use (e.g., `products.yourbrand.com`)
    2. Click **Add Domain**
    
    <Warning>
      Use a subdomain (like `products.yourbrand.com`), not your root domain (`yourbrand.com`). This prevents conflicts with your main website.
    </Warning>
    
    **Recommended subdomain names:**
    - `products.yourbrand.com`
    - `passport.yourbrand.com`
    - `verify.yourbrand.com`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add DNS records">
    After adding your domain, Avelero will provide DNS records that you need to add:
    
    **CNAME record:**
    - Points your subdomain to Avelero's infrastructure
    - Add this record in your domain registrar's DNS settings
    
    **TXT record (for verification):**
    - Proves you own the domain
    - Also added in your DNS settings
    
    **Where to add DNS records:**
    
    The exact location varies by provider, but generally:
    1. Log in to your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.)
    2. Find DNS settings or DNS management
    3. Add new records with the exact values provided by Avelero
    4. Save your changes
    
    <Info>
      DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate, but typically complete within a few hours.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your domain">
    After adding DNS records:
    
    1. Return to the custom domain configuration in Avelero
    2. Click **Verify Domain**
    3. Avelero checks if the DNS records are correctly configured
    
    **Status indicators:**
    
    - **Verified** (green dot): Domain is fully configured and active
    - **Pending** (yellow dot): DNS records are being checked, verification in progress
    - **Verification Needed** (red dot): DNS records are missing or incorrect
    
    If verification fails:
    - Double-check the DNS records match exactly what Avelero provided
    - Wait a few hours for DNS propagation
    - Try verifying again
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing your custom domain

**View domain status**
- The current status is shown in the Custom Domain section
- A badge indicates Verified, Pending, or Verification Needed
- Hover over "Verification Needed" for more details

**Update DNS records**
- If records change, Avelero will notify you
- Click **Configure** to see updated record values
- Update your DNS settings accordingly

**Remove a custom domain**
- Click the three-dot menu (⋯) next to the Configure button
- Select **Remove domain**
- Confirm the removal
- Your passports will revert to Avelero-hosted URLs

<Warning>
  Removing a custom domain will break any QR codes or links using that domain. Only remove if you're replacing it with a new domain or no longer need the feature.
</Warning>

## Theme customization

Customize how your Digital Product Passports look to match your brand identity:

1. Click **Theme** in the sidebar
2. Configure your theme settings:
   - Brand colors
   - Typography
   - Layout preferences
3. Preview changes in real-time
4. Click **Save** to apply your theme

<Info>
  Theme changes apply to all your published Digital Product Passports immediately.
</Info>

## Team management

Invite team members to collaborate on your Digital Product Passports:

### Adding team members

1. Navigate to **Settings > Members**
2. Click **Invite member**
3. Enter the email address
4. Select a role:
   - **Owner**: Full access, can manage billing and delete the brand
   - **Member**: Can create and edit products, limited settings access
5. Click **Send invite**

The invited person will receive an email with instructions to join your brand.

### Managing existing members

- View all team members in the Members list
- See each member's role and status
- Remove members if needed (owners only)
- Change member roles (owners only)

<Note>
  Every brand must have at least one owner. You can't remove the last owner.
</Note>

## Catalog management

Your brand catalog includes reusable data that makes product creation faster:

### Materials catalog

**Settings > Materials**

- Add common materials your products use
- Include country of origin for each material
- Materials appear as options when creating products

### Attributes catalog

**Settings > Attributes**

- Define product characteristics (Color, Size, Material, Style, etc.)
- Create attribute values (Small, Medium, Large for Size)
- Assign colors to values for visual distinction
- Used for product variants

### Manufacturers catalog

**Settings > Manufacturers**

- Add your manufacturing partners
- Include company name and location
- Link manufacturers to products for transparency

### Operators catalog

**Settings > Operators**

- List facilities in your supply chain
- Include facility name, location, and contact
- Used in product journey mapping

### Certifications catalog

**Settings > Certifications**

- Add sustainability certifications
- Include certification body and validity
- Attach to relevant products

### Seasons and tags

**Settings > Seasons** and **Settings > Tags**

- Create seasons or collections (Spring 2024, Summer 2024, etc.)
- Define custom tags for organization
- Use for filtering and grouping products

## Advanced settings

### Integrations

Each integration has specific setup instructions. Click on an integration to configure it.

### Brand deletion

<Warning>
  Brand deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. This option is only available to brand owners.
</Warning>

To delete your brand:

1. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings page
2. In the **Delete Brand** section, click **Delete**
3. Confirm by typing your brand name
4. All products, passports, and data will be permanently deleted

Only delete your brand if you're certain you no longer need it. Consider exporting data first if you want to keep records.

## Best practices

<Accordion title="Keep your brand information up to date">
  Regularly review your brand settings, especially contact email and domain configuration. Outdated information can lead to missed customer inquiries or broken passport links.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Use descriptive brand slugs">
  Choose a slug that clearly represents your brand and is easy to remember. Avoid generic terms or acronyms that customers might not recognize.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Verify custom domains promptly">
  After adding DNS records, verify your domain as soon as possible. Unverified domains won't work for passport URLs and QR codes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Populate your catalog before creating many products">
  Setting up materials, attributes, and operators first saves significant time when creating multiple products. You can select from dropdowns instead of creating each time.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Assign appropriate roles to team members">
  Give team members the minimum access they need. Reserve owner role for trusted administrators who need full control.
</Accordion>

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Custom domain shows "Verification Needed"**

Solution: 
1. Check that DNS records match exactly (copy-paste to avoid typos)
2. Wait 1-4 hours for DNS propagation
3. Use a DNS checker tool to verify records are published
4. Try the verification again

**Problem: Logo appears blurry**

Solution: Upload a higher resolution image. Logos should be at least 400x400 pixels. Larger is better for display on high-DPI screens.

**Problem: Can't change brand slug**

Solution: Ensure the new slug meets requirements (lowercase, no spaces, alphanumeric plus hyphens). The slug may already be taken by another brand.

**Problem: Team member can't see settings**

Solution: Some settings are owner-only. If they need access, an owner must change their role or make specific changes on their behalf.
